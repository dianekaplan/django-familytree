Bob Husband's family:

The Husband side:

  • Leander Wilcox and Amanda Penhallow were born (and later married) in Chautauqua county New York, both with several generations going back in CT, both of their fathers served in the War of 1812, and each of their grandfathers (Richard Penhallow, Stephen Wilcox, Halliday Ingraham, and Abner House) served in the Revolutionary War. Richard Penhallow's father's side immigrated from England in the late 1600's. Stephen Wilcox' ancestors immigrated in the first half of the 1600's (mostly via England & Holland), including two of the passengers on the Mayflower, Francis Cooke and his son John. We came down through John Cooke and Sarah Warren (step-daughter of fellow passenger Richard Warren).
  • Thomas and Elanor Husband were born in Scotland and moved to Canada sometime before 1835. They started their family in Canada before moving to the US in the mid 1830's.
  • Connection: Leander & Amanda's oldest daughter Mary Wilcox married Thomas & Eleanor's oldest son Andrew Husband. Their son Thomas was Bob's father.

The Manning side:

  • William Fitzpatrick came to the US from Ireland in 1847 around the age of 15. His wife Sarah O'Brien was born in the US in 1839, her father from England and her mother from Ireland . Together, they had a daughter named Grace, who was married in 1882, and had Bob's mother Sarah Manning in 1883.

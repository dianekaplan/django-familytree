The Keem girls' house

I drove Vi/Mom, Helen, and Catherine there when Catherine came for a visit to Buffalo some 15-20 years ago. No one was home but they wandered about a bit while reminiscing about riding ponies/horses, sledding down the hill in front of their home (in the street) during the winters, and having one of the first "motor cars" anywhere. It was a wonderful day, and we didn't get arrested for trespassing!
